Tournament Information

Helix Esports

Helix Esports is a gaming lounge in North bergen NJ. the Tournament is run by RJ and the head of Master Hand Gaming Chris. There are two major tournaments held at this venue. The first beginning the weekly "DNA" which happens every thursday at 7:00. The big monthly held at the same place is called "The Forge" which happens on one saturday a month with the doors opening at noon. For all Tournaments on this page their brackets can be found on smash.gg and the images of the maps contain a link with the directions

Platinum Star Games

Platinum Star Games is a video games store in Howell NJ. It is entirely run by Master Hand Gaming with MHG Chris running the streams. The weeklies happen on wednesday and are called "Weenie Hut Jr's" run by the player "Frunkopop". The monthies are also held on saturdays and info regarding them can be found on the Master Hand Gaming Twitter Account.

The Laboratory

The Laboratory is a gaming lounge in Hatboro PA. Their big weekly event is Code Nair:Bairs which happens every friday at 7:30. This event is run by Deli and the Bros_calamity crew.
